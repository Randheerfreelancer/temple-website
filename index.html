        Our History

        According to mythology, the sage Koonamuthappan believed that a strong and amenable deity's presence and blessings were necessary for the welfare of all humanity. So, in order to appease the family deity, he did a harsh penance. Before him, the Goddess quickly materialised. As the son of Shree Parameswara, Chathan Swamy, Koonamuthappan pleaded with the Goddess to provide him the Mantram that would allow him to appear to him. The Goddess instructed him to use the Moola Mantram to summon Chathan to appear before him and the Dhyana Mantram to praise him everyday after being pleased with his penance. Vishnumaya appeared before him as he wished and took the deity with him to Peringottukara. There he consecrated Vishnumaya Chathan Swamy at the place where the Family now lives.
